About Us

Discount Cuts & Services is your licensed and insured full-service landscaping and home services provider.

We are a full-service company that provides every service your lawn and landscape requires to look its best. We have built a reputation for high quality and reliability at affordable prices. At Discount Cuts & Services, we build long-term relationships with our customers and install and maintain their landscape in a way that showcases its unique features. Let us have the opportunity to do the same for yours.

With Discount Cuts, you are provided top-notch service with a smile. We pay attention to detail while working with a servant's heart at a discounted price. The truth is in our name. From lawn care to painting, we provide services to maintain your home or commercial property at an affordable price. If we don't offer a service, we can refer you to a professional affiliate.

Now Expanding: We're excited to bring our services to South Fulton, Chattahoochee Hills, Fairburn, Union City, and Southwest Atlanta—prioritizing these areas for faster response times and local expertise. We continue to serve Paulding, Cobb, Villa Rica, and Douglasville with the same dedication.

Services

We don't only provide landscaping services. We are your go-to company for property maintenance.

Lawn Care

Regular lawn maintenance is the key to keeping any landscape looking its best. We provide top-notch mowing, trimming and edging services. We make your yard the envy of the block and keep your yard looking great all season long.

HOA Common Area Maintenance

We have affordable plans to keep your HOA or condo association entrances and common areas immaculate and inviting.

Landscaping

From building new flower beds and gardens to keeping your trees and shrubs trimmed and clean, we're at your service.

Pressure Washing

Don't hire any old guy with a couple of hundred dollars for a pressure washer to blow holes in your siding or blast the wood off your deck. We are licensed and insured professionals and we want your continued business.

Professional Painting

We provide great interior and exterior painting and staining services at a very affordable price. Do you need a fresh coat of paint? Do you need that deck or fence maintained and sealed? We're your guys! Also, ask us about move-in/move-out maintenance.

Seasonal Cleaning

We can get rid of those pesky leaves, freshen up your mulch or haul that rotting playground equipment away to keep your yard safe and clean. We haul junk of all types. Need to dump an old refrigerator or sofa? Contact us.
